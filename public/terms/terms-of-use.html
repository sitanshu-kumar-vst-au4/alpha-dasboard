10. INSURANCE FUND

The Insurance Fund is held by Alpha5 for the benefit of users, specifically to cover any margin shortfalls which are not covered by standard liquidation procedures.

For a position which falls to or below the necessary margin threshold:

Alpha5 will fully liquidate the position on the market

  1. If the realized amount from fully liquidating a position still results in a shortfall, the balance will be taken from the insurance fund, up to 35% of the Insurance Fund’s total value.
  2. If there is still a shortfall after applying the Insurance Fund, then the balance will be taken from the 50% of the traders with the highest realized and unrealized profit and loss across all of their positions in the past 24 hours, in equal proportions.

Alpha5 reserves a certain trading fee for liquidations for the benefit of the Insurance Fund

The Insurance Fund is held for the benefit of the holders of Alph5 Token (A5T) at any given time. Alpha5 itself does not have any direct interest in the assets held in the Insurance Fund, except to the extent and in the capacity that Alpha5 holds A5T in its own right. Alpha5 will take all reasonable steps to ensure the security of the Insurance Fund and in no event will Alpha5 use the assets in the insurance fund for its own purposes.
